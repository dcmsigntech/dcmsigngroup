Time Remapping and Speed Effects

Importing and Syncing Audio Tracks

Adding Foley and Ambience
7: Color Correction and Grading

Balancing and Enhancing Colors
Using LUTs (Lookup Tables) for Stylistic Looks
Creating Consistent Looks for Scenes
8: Editing for Storytelling
Building Narrative Flow and Emotional Impact
Understanding Scene Structure and Pacing
The Art of Subtle Editing for Dramatic Effect
Using Editing to Build Tension and Suspense
9: Editing Genres and Styles
Editing Techniques for Different Film Genres (Comedy, Drama, Action, etc.)
Exploring Editing Styles in Documentaries, Music Videos, and Advertisements
Analyzing and Deconstructing Editing Choices in Famous Films

Creating Efficient Editing Workflows
Organizing Project Files and Media
Using Proxy Editing for High-Resolution Footage
Archiving and Backing up Projects

Editing Multi-Camera Footage
Handling Special Effects and Green Screen Compositing
Advanced Audio Editing and Mixing
Editing for Virtual Reality (VR) and Immersive Media
13: Editing Ethics and Copyright
Understanding Copyright and Fair Use
Respecting Privacy and Confidentiality
Ethical Considerations in Editing
14: Portfolio and Showreel Development
Assembling a Professional Editing Portfolio
Creating a Showreel to Showcase Editing Skills
Promoting Your Editing Work Online
15: Project: Short Film Editing
A comprehensive project that allows participants to apply their film editing skills to edit a short film from raw footage to the final cut.
